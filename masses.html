PATHWAYS OF GRACE

The Seven Sacraments

✦
🕊

BAPTISM

Celebrated on the 1st Sunday of each month at the 11:00 AM Mass. Contact the parish office to register your child.

🍞

EUCHARIST

Received at every Mass. First Holy Communion preparation runs annually from January to May for children aged 9+.

🙏

RECONCILIATION

Confessions: Saturdays 4:00–5:30 PM and by appointment. Come experience the mercy of God.

✝

CONFIRMATION

Annual Confirmation class for Catholics aged 15 and above. Classes begin each September. Register with the parish secretary.

💍

MATRIMONY

Marriage preparation must begin at least 6 months in advance. Contact the parish office to begin the process.

👐

HOLY ORDERS

Vocations to the priesthood and religious life are discerned with the Diocesan Vocation Director. Contact the Bishop's House.

🫒

ANOINTING OF THE SICK

Available at any time for the seriously ill or dying. Call the parish emergency line for immediate pastoral care: (0812) 567-4455.

FAITH FORMATION

Catechism & RCIA

RCIA (ADULT INITIATION)

MARRIAGE PREP COURSE
